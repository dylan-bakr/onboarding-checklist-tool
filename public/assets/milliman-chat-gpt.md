**Introduction to Milliman ChatGPT and the Generative AI Center**

 

Milliman utilizes its own [[internal
ChatGPT]{.underline}](https://milliman-chatgpt.azurewebsites.net/). As a
general rule, the \"normal\", publicly-available ChatGPT is
**[NOT]{.underline}** to be used.

 

Review the [[Milliman Generative AI
Center]{.underline}](https://milliman.sharepoint.com/sites/MillimanGenAICenter)
for specific \"Dos and Don'ts\" relating to the use of generative AI
**before** using Milliman ChatGPT. This will also explain where use of
generative AI is and is not appropriate. Review other parts of the site
as time and interest allow.
