**Introduction to the Indy Health Exam Reimbursement Request Form**

 

The Indianapolis Health Practice utilizes a standardized form to request
approval for exam study hours and reimbursement related to continuing
education (e.g., actuarial exams, study materials for said exams, or
other continuing education courses).

 

The form can be found
[[here]{.underline}](file:///S:\Resources\Exam%20Reimbursement%20Requests),
at S:\\Resources\\Exam Reimbursement Requests\\Request Form - DO NOT
EDIT.xlsm. There is a brief ReadMe file in the same folder with
instructions.

-   You may notice that there are other subfolders which include records
    for all exam requests; however, these files are all password
    protected and only the CE Committee and Equity Principals will have
    access. [Please avoid editing any files in this
    directory.]{.underline}

-   As noted in the ReadMe, the request form itself should be copied to
    your hard drive or H: drive prior to completing your requests.

 

Linked
[[here]{.underline}](file:///S:\Onboarding%20and%20Training\Training\Exam%20Reimbursement%20Tool\Optional%20Indy%20Health%20webinar_%20Exam%20reimbursement%20request%20tool-20231201_120015-Meeting%20Recording.mp4)
is a recording of a training given on how to use the Request Form.
Expense reports for exam fees or study materials will not be approved
**unless** there is documentation generated from this form. This
includes actuarial and all other exams/certifications covered by the
[[SERC
Policy]{.underline}](https://milliman.sharepoint.com/sites/IndianapolisHealth/Indianapolis%20Health%20Policies/Forms/AllItems.aspx?viewid=6c753414%2D0311%2D4366%2D918b%2D8c05bf42106f&newTargetListUrl=%2Fsites%2FIndianapolisHealth%2FIndianapolis%20Health%20Policies&viewpath=%2Fsites%2FIndianapolisHealth%2FIndianapolis%20Health%20Policies%2FForms%2FAllItems%2Easpx&startedResponseCatch=true&id=%2Fsites%2FIndianapolisHealth%2FIndianapolis%20Health%20Policies%2FStudy%20and%20Exam%20Reimbursement%2FIndy%20Health%20Policy%20%2D%20SERC%20Policy%2020260123%2Epdf&parent=%2Fsites%2FIndianapolisHealth%2FIndianapolis%20Health%20Policies%2FStudy%20and%20Exam%20Reimbursement)
(it does not apply to the Tuition Reimbursement Program).

 

Any questions regarding the Request Form may be directed to the [[SERC
Committee]{.underline}](mailto:indyserc@milliman.com).
